2.13. Independent t-test for Countries & Customer Price¶

The Independent t-test results are significant (p-value very very small)! Therefore, we can reject the null hypothesis in support of the alternative hypothesis.

In [29]:
# filter the data
from scipy import stats
coun = df[(df['Country'] == 'US')]
coun1 = df[(df['Country'] == 'UA')]
# homogeneity
stats.levene(coun['Customer Price'], coun1['Customer Price'])
# Shapiro-Wilk test for normality

stats.shapiro(coun1['Customer Price'])
stats.ttest_ind(coun['Customer Price'], coun1['Customer Price'])

3. Technical Report¶

Missing values were checked, and ten columns have missing value. All of them were filled with None and their values. Firstly, the type of duration was analyzed and 7 Days is preferred by far but for duration of frequency, consumable duration is the most common type. Then the relationship between country and the number of app was examined. First country in the list is U.S.A, and it has remarkable number of applications. The two countries that give the most money to the applications are Japan and Russia. In addition, the price range given to the applications of these two countries is quite high, but the price range given in other countries is low and narrow. Linear Regression is drawn to examine the profit margin and found that the profit margin was about 0.5. Moreover, people who use USD currency to start applications is trial. The Call Recorder - Save&Listen application is used almost 5 times more than other applications. Also, there is two type of ntro for this application. One of them is standard and the other is trial, but trial is much more. The countries that pay the most trial proceeds are Vietnam and Colombia. Also, when we examine the dataset, some applications are completely free and some applications are paid. The paid apps are Interview and Meeting Recorder, Secret Net VPN - Proxy Browser, Call Recorder - Save&Listen, Storico:Instagram story maker, Fax scan pdf and file manager respectively. The most paid applications are Interview and Meeting Recorder and Secret Net VPN - Proxy Browser. Overall, approximately 3,6 billion money spent on apps in 2021 by customers. Apart from these, the dataset was analyzed statistically. There are correlations between customer price and developer proceeds, app apple ID and subscription apple ID, trial price and trial proceed but the other categories are not related to each other. When we apply ttest to countries and customer price, the Independent t-test results are significant (p-value very very small)! Therefore, we can reject the null hypothesis in support of the alternative hypothesis. In one sample z-test , checked customer price and deciding whether sample mean is equal to app name mean or not. According to result, it is equal. Lastly, predicted that a app with 50 Trial Price, and a Develeoper Proceeds of 150, will be approximately 321 of Customer Price for every app. This statistics illustrates us that Customer Price of the applications to the customer is almost twice the developer price which means production price. To sum up, exploratory data analysis was carried out related to MACOSX with keyset dataset, and results were investigated as a visualization, pivot table and statistically. According to queries and graphs, it can be stated that USA is ahead in the application sector as in some other sectors. In addition, it is seen that there is a profit margin here, as in every sector. The most important finding is that people started the applications with a trial version instead of paying directly. The application that spends the most money is mostly for interview management and recording of companies informations. The application sector, without doubt, will develop more with feature engineering technics.
